About M G Whittingham

MG Whittingham is an international best-selling fine art travel photographer from Ottawa, Canada. His photography has a cinematic look that celebrates the vibrancy of life. His images have appeared in books, magazines, CD covers and various advertising campaigns. For more than 10 years, MG Whittingham has helped people transform their home or office into beautiful spaces. Find your perfect piece of wall art today!

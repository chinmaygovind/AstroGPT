Î² Pictoris (beta Pictoris)

Altitude: -14.9°   Azimuth: 163.5°   Direction: South-South-East

Î² Pictoris is a main-sequence star in the constellation of Pictor.

Î² Pictoris visual magnitude is 3.85. Because of its moderate brightness, Î² Pictoris should be easily visible from locations with dark skyes, while it can be barely visible, or not visible at all, from skyes affected by light pollution.

The proper motion of Î² Pictoris is 0.011 arcsec per year in Right Ascension and 0.085 arcsec per year in Declination and the associated displacement for the next 10000 years is represented with the red arrow.
